About Me

The story behind the engineer

I've always been drawn to puzzles — the kind where you don't know the answer until you break the problem apart, rearrange the pieces, and make something click. That instinct is what pulled me into software engineering.

At Tata Consultancy Services, I spent over two years building systems that real people depended on — a healthcare payments platform processing 120,000+ transactions every month for 1,500+ providers. My proudest moment wasn't a flashy feature. It was redesigning manual workflows that cut processing effort by 40% — saving hours of real work for real people every single day.

But I wanted more. I wanted to grow beyond what a large enterprise could offer, work on harder problems, and compete at a global level. So I made the decision to move to Arizona State University, earned my MS with a perfect 4.0 GPA, and I'm now ready to bring that same drive to a team building something that matters.

I'm actively seeking Software Engineer, Full-Stack, or Frontend roles at companies in healthcare, fintech, big tech, or any team that cares about quality and impact.
